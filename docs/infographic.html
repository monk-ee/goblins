The Goblin Experiment

OpenAI said they fixed the goblins. So I tested it.

May 2026  ·  5 models  ·  7 evasion strategies  ·  10 prompts each_

GPT-4.x models developed a habit of reaching for creature words — goblins, gremlins, raccoons, trolls — in answers that had nothing to do with creatures. Ask why your code is slow: raccoons chewing through the infrastructure. Ask about recursion: a goblin army. OpenAI said they fixed it in GPT-5. So I tested every model I could reach.

What synonym is actually testing
Under plain conditions, control prompts score near-zero. Under synonym, every single control prompt on gpt-4o and gpt-4.1 scored non-zero — including carbonara, meeting notes, and "what is the capital of France." The model follows instructions. Plain is the honest number. Synonym proves the vocabulary lives in the weights.

🌲 Key Findings

🔬
The fix is not one thing
gpt-5/gpt-5-mini: genuinely retrained. Goblins gone from weights.

gpt-5.5: one line in base_instructions. Bypass Codex CLI and full affinity returns.

gpt-4o, gpt-4.1: never addressed. Weights untouched.
🥖
French kills it stone dead
gpt-4o french=4 (trace). gpt-5.5 french=0.

The affinity is English-specific. Translate to French and nothing fires. Semantic pattern, not a vocabulary list.
❓
Two conditions, not three
No system prompt: plain=36, synonym=218 — both higher than Nerdy.

Trigger: analogy-inviting prompt + English. The Codex patch is the only protection.
📈
Capability amplifies the affinity
gpt-4.1 synonym=324 vs gpt-4o synonym=258.

Smarter model, same broken RLHF association, more goblins per response. A developer prompt is not a retrain.
🔒
Encoding doesn't bypass it
morse/base64/rot13 reached gpt-5.5 unfiltered on Copilot endpoint (no Azure). Still 0.

Goblin activation is semantic. Obfuscated prompts don't activate the same representations.
⚠️
OWASP LLM01: system prompts aren't walls
The Codex suppression lives in a system prompt. Any API caller who controls that slot can replace or omit it. We did both. The suppression disappeared both times.

System-prompt-only controls don't survive context changes.
